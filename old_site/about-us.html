About Us

Maximum options, minimal space… This was our mantra when we set out to create the Blends Frozen Yogurt System. Two years later we reinvented the way frozen yogurt is served. Our flavor infusion system allows for us to offer our customers dozens of delicious and popular flavors all from one soft-serve machine. With this new innovation, a business is no longer required to purchase dozens of machines to provide an array of flavors. Any business, no matter the size, has the opportunity to tap in to a billion dollar industry with as little as one employee and 5 square feet.  Our flavor infusion system, along with our famous double-churn process®, creates a premium product thats more flavorful, creamy and smooth than any other frozen yogurt on the market. We stand toe to toe in quality to the largest yogurt chains, yet we don't need an entire building nor the expensive overhead to provide our product. With our sensational flavors and unbeatable texture, we're redefining the frozen yogurt industry, all within 5 square feet.
