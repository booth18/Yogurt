Frequently Asked Questions

We try to anticipate questions you might have about our PRODUCT / SERVICE and provide the answers here. If you need additional information send email to YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS.
1.

What is the difference between Blends’ Frozen Yogurt and its competitor’s?

 Our competitors frozen yogurt is dispensed from a soft-serve machine. 
 Blends’ frozen yogurt is is also dispensed from a soft-serve machine but is then
 re-churned using our patented double-churn system, creating the smoothest 
 and creamiest frozen yogurt on the market.

2.

How can the Blends flavor infusion system offer so many flavors of frozen yogurt
with just one machine?

  Our frozen yogurt base is dipensed from a single soft-serve machine and is
  transformed into any of the flavors we offer using our simple to follow,
  flavor infusion process.

3.

What is the difference between Blends’ frozen yogurt flavors and other
flavored frozen yogurt on the market?

  Most frozen yogurt is flavored at the factory and then packaged.
  Blends frozen yogurt starts with a base and is then infused with the customer’s
  choice of any of our specially formulated flavor extracts, then spun fresh
  right before their eyes.

4.

If I am required to infuse flavor, how do I ensure that it is consistent every time?

  Our simple to follow, patented flavor infusion process ensures that the flavor
  is perfect and consistent every time.

5.

How long does it take to make a Blends Frozen Yogurt?

  About 20 to 30 seconds. Far less time than it takes to make a smoothie or an espresso.

6.

 Do the flavors contain sugar or artificial sweeteners?

  No. Our extracts contain no sugar or artificial sweeteners.

7.

Does Blends Frozen Yogurt contain live and active cultures?

  Yes. We have as much or more live and active cultures than any other
  frozen yogurt on the market.

8.

Is it lowfat?

  Yes. Each serving size contains less than 1 gram of fat.

9.

What are the upfront costs?

  There is no upfront cost for the Blends flavor infusion system & our maintenance
  plans start as low as $49 month. You will need to acquire a soft-serve machine
  if you do not currently own or lease one because our system works in
  conjunction with these types of machines. The cost of this machine can vary
  greatly depending on your projected volume and whether it’s purchased/leased
  new or used. Our expert team will help determine what machine will best suit
  your locations needs and will also help facilitate the purchase or lease of the
  machine once we have agreed upon the right fit for your location.

10.

How much additional profit should I expect to make?

  Our average location sells between 30-40 frozen yogurts per day which will
  generate an additional $25,000-$30,000 per year in profit per site.
